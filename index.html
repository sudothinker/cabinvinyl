Cabin Vinyl was born from the idea that sometimes, listening to music should be an intentional experience.

Sometimes you want to appreciate an artist or album, other times you want to listen to a variety of songs and playlists.

Cabin Vinyl combines the experience of browsing albums in a store and owning a personal collection of your favorites. Launch the app, seek out new or old favorites, play the album in its entirety, close the app and enjoy the music.

Live the Cabin lifestyle - Simple, Beautiful, Intentional

Cabin Vinyl is NOT a subscription service, you only pay once. An Apple Music subscription is required.
