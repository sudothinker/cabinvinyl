Put an album on. We’re staying in tonight.

Cabin Vinyl comes from the idea that, sometimes, listening to music should be intentional - appreciating an album in its entirety.

Cabin Vinyl combines the experience of browsing albums in a record store and owning a personal collection of your favorites. Launch the app, seek out new albums or old classics, drag the album to the player, close the app and enjoy the music.

The Cabin lifestyle - Simple, Beautiful, Intentional

The application is not a subscription service, nor does it have in-app purchases. It is pay once and own. It requires an Apple Music subscription to listen to music, but there are no other accounts needed or data collected.
