Cabin Vinyl was born from the idea that sometimes, listening to music should be an intentional experience.

Much like the experience of owning a vinyl record player, we need to intentionally choose music to play, walk over to the player and play it. Sometimes, this is the experience we want, instead of opening your favorite music app and mindlessly listening to auto generated playlists.

Intentionally seek out new or favorite music, play the album in its entirety, get out of the app and enjoy.

Cabin Vinyl combines the experience of browsing albums in a store with owning a personal collection of your favorites.

Live the Cabin lifestyle - Simple, Beautiful, Intentional
